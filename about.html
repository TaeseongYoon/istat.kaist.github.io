About iStat Lab

The Industrial Statistics Lab at KAIST (iStat Lab) aims to advance statistical and machine learning methodologies for analyzing complex, uncertain, and high-dimensional data. Our research bridges traditional statistics and modern AI to enable reliable decision-making in industrial systems, healthcare, and emerging applications of trustworthy AI.

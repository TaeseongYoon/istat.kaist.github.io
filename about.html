About iStat Lab

The Industrial Statistics Lab (iStat Lab) at KAIST develops advanced statistical and machine learning methodologies for complex and uncertain data. We bridge traditional statistical modeling and modern AI to enable reliable, interpretable, and data-driven decision-making across industrial systems, healthcare, and trustworthy AI applications.

Our long-term vision is to build a foundation for the next generation of intelligent systems that are not only accurate but also explainable, robust, and reliable.
